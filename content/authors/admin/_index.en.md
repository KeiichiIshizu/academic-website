## About My Research

I conduct research on geophysical exploration technology that can obtain subsurface information without drilling. Among the various exploration technologies, I focus on "electromagnetic (EM) exploration" that uses electromagnetic induction, governed by Maxwell's equations. My primary exploration targets are **geothermal resources, seafloor metallic mineral resources, and volcanic internal structures**.

In recent years, I have been working intensively on **EM monitoring methods using controlled artificial current sources**. This technology has potential applications in predicting steam explosion eruptions, monitoring geothermal reservoirs, and monitoring CO2 geological sequestration.

I am also developing novel EM survey methods using **drone-mounted receivers** to overcome access limitations in volcanic and geothermal regions.
